Jori Grym

Behavioral Strategist | Behavioral Economics Researcher | Science Communicator

I am a Behavioral Strategist at the Finnish Tax Administration's Headquarters and Legal Unit, and a Behavioral Economics Researcher at Hanken School of Economics. My research focuses on moral judgment and ethical decision-making. I teach consumer and marketing psychology, and regularly appear as a science communicator on Yle's Jälkikaronkka program.
