Jori Grym

Behavioral Strategist | Researcher | Science Communicator

I am a Behavioral Strategist at the Finnish Tax Administration and a Behavioral Economics Researcher at Hanken School of Economics. My research focuses on moral judgment and ethical decision-making. I teach consumer and marketing psychology, and regularly appear as a science communicator on Yle's Jälkikaronkka program and Kulttuuriykkönen Perjantaistudio radio show.
